### 🤨 The Concept
Most productivity apps fail because they lack external motivation. **PROOF** introduces a social layer where:

* **Action requires Evidence:** Tasks flagged as "Public" can only be completed by taking a real-time photo of the result (BeReal-inspired mechanics).
* **Visibility breeds Consistency:** Friends can see your public goals for the day and the "Proof" photos you’ve uploaded.
* **Gentle Nudges:** Friends receive notifications of your pending tasks and can send "nudges" to keep you on track without toxic pressure.
